Why HOUM

To bring back privacy on the internet. For people to have the option of having our data on the internet and yet have it private and not accessed by anyone else. To have a private place we own to keep our private data private.

The consolidation of the ownership of data on the internet is unimaginably high.

You are one of the few billion people who are strongly connected to the internet every day. But everything you do on the internet is done using the products and services offered by a few large companies. They see everything you do and access all your personal data – files, emails, photos, everything! And you don’t own anything on the internet. You are just a user of their products. Exposed and powerless!

We felt that it is wrong to render a few billion people that powerless in the hands of a few large companies. We didn’t feel that’s right. And that’s why we built this product.

To bring back privacy on the internet. To have your data on the internet and yet have it private. Not accessed or owned by any other company. To own a private place on the internet where you can keep your private data actually private.

Above all, to own our online data legally and technically, for the first time!

Benefits to Owners

Privacy

Everything we do on the internet today is tracked by some companies. Our photos, private chats, personal emails - all these are read, analysed and categorized by them, even though they are quite personal.

Building a digital houm with houm.me will give you a domain and a server of your own. This means, you own a small piece of the internet that is entirely private to you, and no other company or its software can access what you store there – giving you true privacy.

Ownership

The concept of owning a piece of the internet individually is not familiar to us, because we have been using products offered free by other companies. Thus, they became the owners of everything on the internet. We became their users. Even worse, they own all our digital assets now because we stored it on their servers under their domains.

You can buy a domain and a server – your digital houm and store your digital assets on your server under your domain. Then, you become the true owner of your digital assets, and no one else can peep.
